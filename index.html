Multi-Disciplinary Engineering

The Integrated Engineering Program (IGEN) offers a multidisciplinary undergraduate engineering education. IGEN graduates are trained in the six core engineering subjects (CHBE, CIVL, EECE, MECH, MINE, MTRL) over the first two years of their degree. After that they specialize in a major/minor pairing of complimentary subjects.



Entrepreneurial Development

All integrated engineers are required to take IGEN 340 – Technology Entrepreneurship which teaches the fundamentals of market analysis and business strategy and execution.



Inspiring Innovation

At UBC, Integrated Engineering is synonymous with Innovation. Students in the IGEN program are pushed to forget all preconceived notions about today’s technological problems. Then through idea incubation, research, and project development students are taught to hypothesize and test unique solutions to real world problems. Innovation and invention don’t need to be incremental, iterative processes that happen in a laboratory. They can be products of imagination and intuition backed by a engineering common sense and a strong will to succeed.
