Your safety is our business.


Our business is committed to delivering our goal through our core values. Our values are more than just statements; they define our reasons for being in business and what it means to be part of our Specialised Team.

By investing in our own people we are able to offer expert advice and support to our customers. All our sales and technical staff undergo continuous product training. Our team also has qualified Occupational Health & Safety practitioners. We will continue to provide training to our team so that we can offer practical support and sensible advice. Our team of highly qualified experts work in partnership with you to offer practical, hassle-free services to ensure that your work place is safe and compliant.

As market leaders we offer the widest range of PPE in the East African region. We have an impressive product range and hold large stocks. Furthermore, the products are available through a large network of distributors, ensuring you can get what you need when you need it. If you have got a large order or bespoke requirements then come and talk to us about a deal.
